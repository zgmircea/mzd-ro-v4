about me

I was born in the heart of Transolvania on 1987. I have a Bachlor in Computes Science but my ture passion has been design since 2004. It’s beocome a significant part of my life, particularly webdesign but it also merged with my love for frontend development.

I’ve been to a series of national contests up until 2006 and won diffrent types of prizes for web design & developent.

I’m currently working in a company called Imprezzio in Cluj-Napoca. I’ve also worked in a design boutique but I’ve lived a secret life and still did freelanceing. Due to the various areas of projects I’ve worked with I feel a certain ease adapting to new experiences.

I have been working mainly on projects that involve simple and modular design, efficient seo-oriented HTML and light, optimized CSS.

I’m pixel-perfect detail-oriented, very motivated, self-educated and I am constantly seeking to increase my professional skills and achieve an ever higher level of expertise in my domain, while concentrating on delivering the most efficient and effective solutions.

I’m always looking for the chance to work on innovative and ambitious projects and products

Socially I’m an easy going guy, with a big sense of humor.
